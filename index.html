Where the function adds value

Consumer evidence has the most leverage early.

The influence of a measurement is largest when direction, formulation, price, and claims are still open. Moving evidence to those points keeps decisions informed while they are still inexpensive to revise.

How we work

The method follows the decision.

Each engagement begins from the decision in question and the specific uncertainty blocking it. A method is chosen to reduce that uncertainty, and the result is returned as an estimate a team can act on, with its assumptions and error stated.

Frame

State the decision and the uncertainty to resolve.

Measure

Estimate likely response, with appropriate error.

Act

Return a result the team can use, and update it as evidence arrives.

Worked example

Aurea
Botanical adaptogen tonic · a concept

A daily tonic for calm and focus: reishi, ashwagandha, and honey, taken by dropper. One concept runs through every capability below so each has a concrete referent. No product exists; it is a device for illustration.

AdaptogensReishiAshwagandhaHoneyCalm & focusDropper doseDaily use

Simple to sip. Harder to specify.

On the surface, a tonic. Underneath sit a set of measurable consumer questions, each one a point where a launch can miss.

01 Who is the primary buyer?
02 Do adaptogens attract or deter?
03 Is the taste repeatable?
04 Which claims hold up?
05 What price holds intent?
06 Does use persist?

Methods & infrastructure

A shared analytical layer supports the suite.

The capabilities above draw on a common set of analytical and behavioral methods.

Advanced & Predictive Analytics

Regression, choice models, forecasting, and machine learning applied to estimation.

Behavioral Science

Jobs-to-be-done, decision biases, and choice architecture; revealed behavior alongside stated preference.

GenAI & Synthetic MethodsAdvanced

LLM-assisted coding of qualitative data and synthetic pre-tests, validated against human samples.

Decision Systems & Reporting

Estimates delivered with their assumptions, error, and the decision they inform.

Evidence Standards

Samples sized to the decision, pre-specified success criteria, and effect sizes reported with confidence.
