Stage by stage

What we decide together, at each gate.

The same lifecycle, walked one stage at a time — the decision we improve, the uncertainties we resolve for the hydration + creatine product, and the output you receive.

Pre-IPR

Is this worth pursuing at all?

We help make the go / no-go into IPR — proving the opportunity is real, defining the bar to beat, and finding a price that can actually work.

Uncertainties we resolve
  • Is hydration + creatine a meaningful opportunity or a niche idea, and which consumers are most open to it?
  • Which brands are the real benchmarks, and what taste, claims, and price ranges are already accepted?
  • What price holds intent, and where does it drop sharply?
OpportunityBenchmarkPrice corridor

You receive a decision-ready opportunity read: the winning need-state, the competitive bar, and a defensible price corridor to design toward — with a clear recommendation to advance or stop.

Discovery

What exactly should we build — and claim?

We help choose the concept route and prototype plan, so R&D builds the right hypotheses first and Marketing knows which claims are credible.

Uncertainties we resolve
  • Which route is most motivating — hydration + performance, + recovery, daily creatine made easy, or clean active-lifestyle?
  • Which claims are believable, motivating, and compliant?
  • Which features matter most, and which are not worth overbuilding?
Concept & claimsPrototype plan

You receive a prioritized prototype brief: the lead concept, the claim set to pursue, and the hypotheses to build first — with the trade-offs made explicit.

Feasibility

Can we make it good enough to win?

We help decide which prototypes advance — screening quickly, diagnosing sensory precisely, and modeling what actually drives liking and intent.

Uncertainties we resolve
  • Which prototypes clear the sensory and mixability bar, and which flavors are immediate rejects?
  • What drives liking — sweetness, tartness, authenticity, mouthfeel, aftertaste, mixability?
  • What drives intent — creatine credibility, hydration, taste, convenience, price — and does the pack support repeat use?
ScreenSensory DoEPackaging

You receive a driver map and formulation direction: which levers to adjust next, the trade-offs between function and taste, and a pack that reduces dose confusion.

Development

Is it ready — and can we prove it?

We help confirm the product is at target and defensible — optimizing to the profile, then substantiating claims, superiority, and on-pack guidance.

Uncertainties we resolve
  • Is the smallest formulation change producing meaningful lift, without new problems?
  • Which claims can we support, and where should Marketing avoid overclaiming?
  • Do consumers understand dose, water volume, and frequency well enough to avoid misuse?
OptimizeClaimsSuperiorityInstructions

You receive a launch-readiness evidence pack: the final profile, the substantiated claim set, the superiority/parity picture, and instructions proven to be understood.

Commercial.

Will it convert — and land in real life?

We help decide whether to scale, refine, or pause — instrumenting the funnel and reading the first 90 days of real use as one connected signal.

Uncertainties we resolve
  • Where do consumers drop — page, claim comprehension, price, cart, first use, repeat?
  • Are early complaints about taste, mixability, confusion, or expectations?
  • Are early consumers using it as intended and repeating?
Funnel30/60/90 pulse

You receive a launch health readout: the drop-off points, the content that improves conversion, the top dissatisfaction drivers, and a clear scale / refine / pause call.

Post-IPR

What do we learn — and fix next?

We help set the improvement roadmap — turning reviews and observed habits into prioritized fixes and the next opportunity.

Uncertainties we resolve
  • What drives 1-star vs 5-star reviews, and are consumers misunderstanding the product?
  • Which themes are growing over time, and what should we prioritize?
  • How is it really used — and what routines drive repeat purchase?
VOC + reviewsHabits & practices

You receive a prioritized fix list and habit map: what to praise, what to repair first, and where the next adjacent product could go.

Selected deep dives

Five capabilities, shown in a little more detail.

The less obvious ones — where the method connections matter most. Figures shown are illustrative.

Deep dive · 01

Opportunity + Delta-to-Win

We size the need-states, choose the lane worth winning, then translate consumer language into measurable targets R&D can build and Marketing can claim.

Which need-state is big and under-served?
Who is the first consumer to win?
How much better must we be to justify switching?
Supports the decision: what "winning" requires — before heavy prototyping spend.
Deep dive · 02

Price Corridor & Dynamics

A corridor, not a single number. We map the ladder consumers already know, quantify the acceptable range and break point, and translate it into guardrails by segment.

What price is acceptable for hydration + creatine?
Do consumers pay more for creatine, flavor, or clean-label?
Which segments are value- vs performance-sensitive?
Supports the decision: the price corridor that guides formulation, pack count, and claims.
Deep dive · 03

Consumer-Linked DoE

A designed experiment that connects formulation levers directly to consumer outcomes — so R&D changes are chosen by their effect on liking and intent, not by guesswork.

What actually drives liking vs perceived efficacy?
Which levers trade off against each other?
What's the smallest change with the biggest lift?
Supports the decision: which formulation levers to move next.
Deep dive · 04

Usage Instructions & On-Pack Guidance

We test whether people actually understand dose, water volume, and frequency — and which format (icons, short steps, QR video, warnings) prevents the misuse that fuels bad reviews.

Do consumers know how much water to use?
Do they understand creatine frequency?
Which instruction format is understood fastest?
Supports the decision: the on-pack guidance that prevents misuse and returns.
Deep dive · 05

Voice of Consumer + Ratings & Reviews

Ratings and reviews are one major input into a broader VOC system. We separate what earns praise from what drives 1-star ratings, flag misunderstanding, and track which themes are growing.

What drives 1-star vs 5-star reviews?
Are consumers misunderstanding the product?
Which themes are growing over time?
Supports the decision: which fixes to prioritize first.
